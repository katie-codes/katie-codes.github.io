INFOGRAPHIC

This was a really fun freelance Graphic Design job to create a set of Travel Consultant characters for use in training materials including this Infographic for the Flight Centre Learning Centre. I consulted with the trainers on what the onboarding process was for consultants and re-created the timeline in this Infographic so they could easily follow along with the process of training and starting in store as a novice consultant. This work continued onto a short animated video, please see next item from the home page.
