Paper planes

I was asked to come up with a concept and set of graphic elements for new communication strategy for the Flight Centre Air team. The Paper plane concept was simple and effective. I incorporated existing brand colours with a new orange to make it bright and modern. The dotted display font lent itself easily to making the tail of a plane. All the additional dotted lines and plane graphical elements were provided to the brand leader, along with a tutorial in Canva and Mailchimp, as being self-sufficient for a quick turn around in comms was of utmost importance.

Katie Patterson 2020
