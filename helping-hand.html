Helping Hand Dashboard

The Helping Hand dashboard was one of my favourite collaborative projects completed in Infinity. We wanted to get the most out of every interaction we have with retail agents, especially when they reach out to us. In order to put some information into the hands of a wholesale consultant that will help them quickly identify opportunities while they are on the phone that is targeted and personalised to the retail consultant.

By using transactional data in real time in this trial, we were able to drive stronger engagement and more meaningful conversations with our retail partners and ultimately improved our quote to booking rate by 8%. This project lead the way for all the future projects I would lead including the introduction a chatbot, single consultant ID in web analytics, and requirements gathering for a CRM.

Katie Patterson 2020
